Five largest protests in US History

The tradition of protests for progressive change continues.

Here are the country's top five protests in terms of attendance.

  1. George Floyd Protests nationwide with 26 million in attendance
  2. Women's March in 2017 with 5.6 million
  3. March for our lives a student protest in 2018, drew 2 million people.
  4. 2018 Women's March drew 1.5 million protesters.
  5. March on Washington for Gay, lesbian, Bi and Equal Rightsdrew about 1 million people in 1991.
